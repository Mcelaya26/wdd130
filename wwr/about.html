To provide safe, empowering, and unforgettable river experiences while fostering respect for wild waterways, teamwork, and personal growth.

History

Wild Current Rafting Co. began in 2014 after founder Caleb Rivers noticed a growing gap in the rafting industry: plenty of thrill-focused outfitters, but very few that truly supported beginners and families who wanted to learn the river rather than just survive it. Starting with two rafts, a borrowed trailer, and weekends only, Caleb ran trips himself while Maya helped design safety training and Evan volunteered to manage logistics.
