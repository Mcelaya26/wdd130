About Wild Current Rafting Co.

Mission Statement

To provide safe, empowering, and unforgettable river experiences while fostering respect for wild waterways, teamwork, and personal growth.

Why They Do What They Do

Wild Current believes rivers have a unique way of teaching people—how to trust, how to work together, and how to stay present in moments that matter. The team isn’t just there to guide rafts downstream; they’re there to help people overcome fear, connect with nature, and walk away feeling stronger than when they arrived. For the founders and guides, rafting isn’t about conquering the river—it’s about learning to move with it. They do what they do because they’ve seen how a single day on the water can change how someone sees themselves, their limits, and the world around them.

History

Wild Current Rafting Co. began in 2014 after founder Caleb Rivers noticed a growing gap in the rafting industry: plenty of thrill-focused outfitters, but very few that truly supported beginners and families who wanted to learn the river rather than just survive it. Starting with two rafts, a borrowed trailer, and weekends only, Caleb ran trips himself while Maya helped design safety training and Evan volunteered to manage logistics. Word spread quickly about the company’s patient guides and safety-first approach. Within three seasons, Wild Current expanded into a full-time operation with a dedicated training program and a loyal base of returning guests.
